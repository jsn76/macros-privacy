Privacy Policy

Macros — Macro Tracker · Effective 21 June 2026

Macros — Macro Tracker (“the app,” “we,” “us”) respects your privacy. This policy explains what we collect and how we use it.

Information we collect

  • Account information: If you create an account, we collect your email address to sign you in and sync your data.
  • Health & nutrition data: Food entries, macro and calorie targets, and related logs you enter. This stays associated with your account and is used solely to provide the app’s tracking features.
  • Diagnostics: We may collect anonymous crash and performance data to fix bugs and improve stability.

How we use your information

We use your data only to operate and improve the app — to save your logs, sync across your devices, and diagnose problems. We do not sell your personal data or share it with third parties for advertising.

Data storage & deletion

Your data is stored securely. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us.
