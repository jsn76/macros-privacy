Common questions

Do MyRecipes and MyMacros share an account?

Yes. Both apps use the same sign-in, so one account works in both. A Pro subscription in either app unlocks the other one too.

How do I manage or cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are handled by the App Store. Open Settings on your iPhone, tap your name, then Subscriptions — or manage them from the plan card on the app's Profile page. Cancelling keeps your access until the end of the paid period.

A scanned food's numbers look wrong.

Barcode data comes from public food databases and the occasional product record is out of date. You can tap any logged food to adjust its values, and re-scanning often picks up a corrected record. Feel free to email us the product name and barcode so we can look into it.

How do I delete my account?

In either app, go to Profile → Account → Delete account. This permanently removes your account and all your data from our servers.
